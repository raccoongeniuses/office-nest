This document outlines the future vision for **Office Nest**. These features are currently **out of scope** for the initial MVP but are planned for subsequent phases.

## Phase 2: Integrations & Notifications
- [ ] **WhatsApp Integration**:
    -   Receive notifications for leave requests and approval status directly in WhatsApp.
    -   "Approve/Reject" quick actions from WhatsApp messages.
- [ ] **Email Notifications**:
    -   Automated emails for request submission, approval, and rejection.
    -   Weekly summary emails for managers.
- [ ] **Calendar Sync**:
    -   Google Calendar / Outlook integration to automatically block out approved leave dates.
    -   "Team Leave" calendar subscription link (.ics).

## Phase 3: Advanced HR Features
- [ ] **Payroll Integration**:
    -   Export approved leave and reimbursement data in CSV/Excel formats compatible with payroll software.
    -   Automated calculation of unpaid leave deductions.
- [ ] **Analytics Dashboard**:
    -   Visual charts for leave trends (e.g., peak leave months).
    -   Expense reports by category and department.
    -   Employee efficiency/attendance insights.
- [ ] **Department Management**:
    -   Hierarchy Visualization.
    -   Department-specific leave quotas per year.

## Phase 4: Expansion
- [ ] **Mobile Application**:
    -   React Native app for employees to submit requests on the go.
    -   Camera integration for easier receipt scanning.
- [ ] **Performance Management**:
    -   KPI tracking and quarterly reviews.
    -   1:1 meeting log between managers and employees.
- [ ] **Multi-Tenancy**:
    -   Support for multiple organizations with isolated data.
